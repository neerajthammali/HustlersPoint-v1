# **App Name**: Hustlerspoint

## Core Features:

- Blog: Blog of research articles and productivity blogs for 18 to 24 years peer group who wanna self devolp and grow
- Stories: Import and display stories of hustlers from LinkedIn, reddit, product hunt and more relative
- Services Showcase: Dedicated section to promote freelancers, students, engineers, and SaaS builders, featuring their profiles and services.
- Trending News: TRENDING NEWS FROM ALL PLATFROM AND TRENDING THINGS Daily news
- Community Discussion Forums: Allow users to SHARE AND DISCUSS ABOUT THERE INTERESTS

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A VOILET (#7237C5) to convey trust and innovation.
- Background color: A light gray (#F5F5F5) to provide a clean and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: A VIBRANT BLUE (#29ABE2) to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Body font: 'MONSTERT', a humanist sans-serif providing modern clarity.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk', a techy sans-serif that fits the content.
- Use modern, flat icons to represent categories and actions, ensuring clarity and visual appeal.
- Implement a clean, CARD-based layout to ensure content is well-organized and easy to navigate.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and provide feedback on interactions.
